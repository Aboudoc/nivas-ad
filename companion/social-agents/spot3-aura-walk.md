# Agent social spot 3 — Single clip: the aura walk (sexy-funny parody)

- **Concept**: the classic Thai "sexy-funny" TVC mechanic — a glamorous
  perfume-commercial moment comically undercut inside the same frame. The glam is
  the joke; the reveal is the punchline; the phone is the reason for the aura.
- **One part only**: a single 8 s clip, self-sufficient for Reels/TikTok.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0 · **Format**: 9:16 · 8 s · continuous shot
- **Tone guard**: cheeky and glamorous, never vulgar — fully clothed, the comedy
  carries the sexiness (that's the Thai way).
- **Post-production**: super + logo after the wink (never generated):

  ```
  ออร่าของเอเจนท์ที่สต็อกไม่มีพลาด
  ```
  *"The aura of an agent whose stock never misses."*
  + Nivas Companion logo. Sound: over-the-top perfume-ad orchestral swell that
  scratches to a stop on the fan reveal, then resumes for the wink.

## Prompt

```
Vertical 9:16, single continuous 8-second shot, photorealistic, Thai TV commercial
sexy-funny parody style — a glamorous perfume-commercial moment comically undercut.

A stunning Thai woman real-estate agent in an elegant fitted dress walks in dramatic
slow motion through a luxury Bangkok condo lobby, hair flowing in the wind,
confident supermodel walk, golden light, subtle lens flare. The doorman and a
waiting client couple turn their heads in awe, jaws dropping; a security guard fans
himself with his cap. As she passes, the frame reveals the source of the "wind": a
cheap plastic standing fan taped onto a rolling office chair, dragged along behind
her by a sweaty male assistant scurrying in fast motion, struggling to keep up. She
never breaks the glamorous slow-motion walk, then flashes her smartphone toward
camera with a cheeky wink.

Contrast of exaggerated slow motion against the assistant's fast comic scurry,
parody of luxury perfume ads, warm golden grade with punchy Thai TVC colors, slight
film grain. Tasteful, playful, fully clothed. No text, no logos.
```
