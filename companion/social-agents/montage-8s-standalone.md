# Agent social spot — Single-clip variant: the 8 s spot

- **Usage**: when a single 8 s clip has to do the job (story, teaser, tight budget).
  No new prompt: this is an **editing recipe** on top of Clip 1.
- **The twist stays the contrast**: real-world chaos → calm app.

## Recipe

1. Generate **Clip 1** (`clip1-chaos-motosai.md`) — keep the first 5 seconds
   (peak panic: the sticky note flying away).
2. At **t = 5 s**, hard cut to a **real screen recording** of Nivas Companion
   (2 s: the available-units list, one tap, "sent").
3. At **t = 7 s**, 1 s end card:

   ```
   นิวาส คอมแพเนียน — ครบ จบ ในมือถือ
   ```
   *"Nivas Companion — complete, done, on your phone"* + logo.

4. Sound design: real Thai VO from Clip 1 over the first 5 seconds, one "pop" SFX on
   the cut to the app, silence on the end card. Burned-in Thai subtitles (sound is
   often off on mobile).
