# Agent social spot 4 — Single clip: the lakorn almost-kiss (soap-opera parody)

- **Concept**: the OTHER great Thai sexy-comedy register — the **lakorn parody**
  (Thai prime-time soap opera): over-acted romance, golden backlight, wind, string
  music, the classic almost-kiss… snapped in half by professionalism. The joke:
  the only thing that makes her heart race is a closing deal.
- **One part only**: a single 8 s clip, self-sufficient for Reels/TikTok.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0 · **Format**: 9:16 · 8 s · continuous shot
- **Tone guard**: romantic tension played completely straight (that's what makes it
  funny), fully clothed, never vulgar.
- **Post-production**: super + logo on the frozen pucker (never generated):

  ```
  หล่อก็หล่อ… แต่ดีลต้องมาก่อนค่ะ
  ```
  *"Handsome, sure… but the deal comes first."*
  + Nivas Companion logo. Sound: sweeping lakorn string music that cuts dead on the
  phone buzz, one dry "ping!", then a cheerful ukulele sting on the contract.

## Prompt

```
Vertical 9:16, single continuous 8-second shot, photorealistic, Thai prime-time
soap-opera (lakorn) parody inside a TV commercial — melodrama played completely
straight, then comically snapped.

Golden sunset floods a Bangkok penthouse. A devastatingly handsome Thai male client,
soap-opera hero look, collar slightly open, leans slowly toward the beautiful Thai

empty air, lips still puckered, blinking at camera.

Lakorn visual codes: warm golden backlight, soft glow, lens flare, over-dramatic
acting; then sharp comic timing on the snap. Punchy Thai TVC colors, slight film
grain. Tasteful, fully clothed. No text, no logos.
```
