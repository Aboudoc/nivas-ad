# Website hero — Luxury set: Sukhumvit avenue at night

- **Usage**: homepage background candidate, luxury-grade set. Sukhumvit at night —
  but shot like a perfume ad, not a dashcam: elevated view, traffic reduced to
  continuous silky light streams (a constant flow loops seamlessly, unlike
  readable individual cars).
- **Look**: French luxury perfume-commercial cinematography over neon Bangkok.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0 · **Format**: 16:9 · 8 s · locked-off · loop
- **Export**: compressed muted mp4 + webm, ~1–2 MB target.

## Prompt

```
Ultra-photorealistic cinematic static wide shot, locked-off camera, no camera
movement, no cuts, single continuous 8-second shot, 16:9. French luxury perfume
commercial cinematography: soft optical bloom, gentle atmospheric haze, champagne
highlights, deep elegant shadows, immaculate composition.

Bangkok night, elevated view down Sukhumvit avenue: a glowing canyon of condo
towers and warm neon signs, the elevated BTS track drawing a dark elegant line
above the road. Traffic is abstracted into continuous silky streams of golden and
ruby light flowing along the avenue — long-exposure style, no individual vehicles
readable. Windows glow warm amber against deep teal-blue night; gentle tropical
haze softens the distance.

Only slow, constant motion inside the frame: the smooth uninterrupted light
streams, a subtle shimmer of neon reflections, faint drifting haze. No readable
cars, no train passing, no people, no flickering signs. Loop-friendly: the light
flow is constant and continuous, first and last frame nearly identical. High
detail, filmic contrast. No text, no logos.
```
