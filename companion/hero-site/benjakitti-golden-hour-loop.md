# Website hero — Luxury set: Benjakitti Park at first golden hour

- **Usage**: homepage background candidate, luxury-grade set. The iconic Bangkok
  contrast: the wetland park mirror ringed by glass towers, at the very first
  minutes of golden hour (late afternoon, long soft light).
- **Look**: French luxury perfume-commercial cinematography — the grade carries the
  brand (soft teal shadows, warm amber highlights = the app's tropic palette).
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0 · **Format**: 16:9 · 8 s · locked-off · loop
- **Export**: compressed muted mp4 + webm, ~1–2 MB target — it autoplays on every
  visit.

## Prompt

```
Ultra-photorealistic cinematic static wide shot, locked-off camera, no camera
movement, no cuts, single continuous 8-second shot, 16:9. French luxury perfume
commercial cinematography: soft optical bloom, gentle atmospheric haze, champagne
highlights, deep elegant shadows, immaculate balanced composition.

Bangkok's Benjakitti Park at the very first minutes of golden hour, late

light, lush wetland grasses and lotus beds in the foreground, elevated walkways

Only slow, calm motion inside the frame: a gentle shimmer on the water, grasses
swaying in a light breeze, slow drifting clouds. No people, no birds, no vehicles.
Loop-friendly: first and last frame nearly identical. Warm amber highlights, soft
teal shadows, filmic contrast, high detail. No text, no logos.
```
