# Website hero — Variant: Wat Arun sunset sky (matches the Nivas Global still)

- **Usage**: homepage background candidate in the style of the current Nivas Global
  hero still (Wat Arun spire, pastel sunset sky). Almost-static: only clouds drift.
  Left third kept as open sky for the headline.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0
- **Format**: 16:9 · 8 s · fixed shot · loop
- **Validation**: clouds drift slowly and nothing else moves; headline space stays clear.

## Prompt

```
Ultra-photorealistic cinematic static wide shot, locked-off tripod camera, no camera
movement, no cuts, single continuous 8-second shot, 16:9.

Bangkok at sunset: the ornate spire of Wat Arun temple rising in warm golden light
against a vast dramatic sky of pastel pink, lavender and orange clouds, the low dark
silhouette of the city skyline along the bottom edge. Composition: the temple spire
sits center-right; the left third of the frame is open sky, clear space for a
headline. The only motion inside the frame: clouds drifting very slowly, a subtle
warm shimmer of the last sunlight on the spire. No boats, no birds, no vehicles, no
people.

Soft pastel color grade matching a warm tropical dusk, gentle filmic contrast, high
detail. Loop-friendly: the cloud field looks continuous, first and last frame nearly
identical. No text, no logos.
```
