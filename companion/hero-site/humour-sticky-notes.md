# Website hero — HUMOUR version 2: the sticky-note storm

- **Usage**: second humorous candidate for the homepage background. Single-character
  gag, classic Thai TVC timing: chaos → long deadpan look to camera → product gesture.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0
- **Format**: 16:9 · 8 s · fixed shot (no camera movement, no cuts)
- **Post-production**: super to burn in (never generated):
  **« มีวิธีที่ง่ายกว่านะ »** (*"There's an easier way, you know"*) + Nivas Companion logo.
  Muted on the website.

## Prompt

```
Static wide shot, fixed camera, no cuts, single continuous 8-second shot, 16:9,
photorealistic, Thai TV commercial comedy style.

A tiny cluttered street-side real-estate agent desk in Bangkok, bright tropical
daylight, saturated colors. A Thai woman agent around 30 sits behind a desk buried
under hundreds of pink and yellow sticky notes — stuck on the wall, the desk, her
motorcycle helmet, the electric fan, even her shirt. The oscillating desk fan turns
toward the wall: a gust blows a whole wave of sticky notes into the air like
confetti. She freezes and stares straight into the camera with a long deadpan look
while notes rain down around her... then slowly raises her smartphone next to her
face with a tiny proud smile.

Thai TVC comedy timing: chaos, deadpan look to camera, product gesture. Warm skin
tones, punchy saturated colors, slight film grain. No text, no logos.
```
