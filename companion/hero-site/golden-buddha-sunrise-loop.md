- **Look**: French luxury perfume-commercial cinematography; the gold itself is the
  champagne highlight. Respectful and still — no gimmicks around a sacred figure.
- **Model**: Seedance 2.0 · **Format**: 16:9 · 8 s · locked-off · loop
- **Export**: compressed muted mp4 + webm, ~1–2 MB target.
